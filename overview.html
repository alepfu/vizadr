VizADR
Welcome to VizADR!
What?
This tool visualizes collected data about adverse drug reactions (ADR)
from the The Canada Vigilance Adverse Reaction Online Database.

Why?
ADRs are one of the leading causes of morbidity and mortality in
health care (source).

How?
This chart shows the collected reports for every disease class (SOC).
Move cursor over bars to get additional information.
Click on labels or bars to load a detailed analysis.
